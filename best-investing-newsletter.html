What are the wildcards exactly?
Every morning Stratum selects the 2 non-Chinese, non-Arabic sources where the most significant signal divergence is occurring that day. It could be Japanese, Korean, Russian, French, German — whatever market is generating the most actionable intelligence that morning. It changes daily.
What time does it arrive?

What is Congressional Trading Watch?
US lawmakers must disclose stock trades under the STOCK Act. Stratum monitors these real filings and flags when politicians are buying or selling before major policy decisions — energy stocks before pipeline votes, defense stocks before military appropriations.
