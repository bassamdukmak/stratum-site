What Is
Stratum Wealth?

The Mission

Stratum Wealth was built on a simple idea: the kind of market intelligence that moves institutional money shouldn't be locked behind six-figure Bloomberg terminals or expensive research subscriptions.

We deliver a daily market intelligence briefing, every morning at 9:10 AM Eastern, covering the signals, sectors, and stories that matter. Written in plain English. No Wall Street jargon. No spin.

What You Get

  • A 10-section daily intelligence report covering macro, trade ideas, geopolitical risks, and more
  • Regional Signal Radar, 1 to 4 cross-language signals daily from any country, surfacing stories before they reach Western press
  • Top 5 ranked actionable trade ideas with conviction scores and time horizons
  • Bias-aware reporting, we flag where Western and non-Western narratives diverge
  • Delivered to your inbox every morning, 365 days a year

Who It's For

Stratum is built for everyday people who want to understand what's actually moving markets, not for hedge fund managers who already have research teams. If you're new to investing, curious about markets, or just want to stay informed without spending hours reading financial news, Stratum is for you.

The Tiers

  • Free Trial, 7 days of daily Plus reports including the Sunday FA Deep Dive.
  • Plus ($19.99/mo), Daily reports every morning at 9:10 AM ET, including the Financial Advisor Deep Dive layer. No weekly summaries or congressional trading.
  • Quant ($49.99/mo), Everything in Plus, plus weekly summaries (Sundays), monthly wrap-ups, and Congressional & Political Trading Watch.

// FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Is Stratum really free?
Yes. Every new signup gets a 7-day free trial of Plus — daily reports including the Financial Advisor Deep Dive, delivered every morning at 9:10 AM ET. After 7 days the trial ends automatically. No charges, ever.
When do reports arrive?
Reports are delivered every morning at 9:10 AM Eastern Time. Free Trial users receive a full report every day during their 7-day window. Plus and Quant subscribers receive a report every day, ongoing.
What's the difference between Free, Plus, and Quant?
Free Trial is 7 days of full Plus access — daily reports plus the Financial Advisor Deep Dive. Plus ($19.99/mo) is the same daily flow, ongoing. Quant ($49.99/mo) is Plus plus weekly summaries (Sundays), monthly wrap-ups, and Congressional & Political Trading Watch.
What is the Financial Advisor Deep Dive?
A dedicated layer analyzing key stocks in depth, covering earnings trends, valuation metrics, analyst targets, margins, ownership, and a final verdict (STRONG BUY, BUY, BUILD SLOWLY, WAIT, or AVOID). Delivered as part of the daily report for Plus and Quant subscribers, and included in the 7-day Free Trial.
What is Congressional Trading Watch?
A section that surfaces publicly disclosed U.S. congressional trading activity (filed under the STOCK Act) and notes timing patterns relative to public events. Available on the Quant tier. We do not allege or imply illegal activity by any individual; we report on public disclosures.
What languages do you analyze?
Our Regional Radar surfaces 1 to 4 cross-language signals daily from any country in the world. China, the Middle East, India, Brazil, Nigeria, Korea, Indonesia, anywhere with a major fresh story. We monitor sources in English, Mandarin, and Arabic, looking for divergences between what Western media reports and what regional sources are saying. Often the most valuable signals come from these gaps. Some days we ship 4, some days we ship 2. Quality over quota.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your billing portal with no penalties. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.
Is this financial advice?
No. Stratum Wealth is an informational and educational service only. We are not a registered investment advisor, broker-dealer, or financial planner. Nothing in our reports — including verdicts, conviction scores, and trade ideas — is a personalized recommendation. Past results we reference are selected examples and are not indicative of future performance. Always consult a qualified financial professional in your jurisdiction before making investment decisions.
How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Click "Manage Subscription" in any report email, or visit stratumwealth.ca and use the manage subscription link in the footer. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel directly from there.
I didn't receive my report. What should I do?
Check your spam or promotions folder first. If you still can't find it, make sure you confirmed your email subscription.
