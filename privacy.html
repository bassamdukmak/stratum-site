Privacy
Policy.

Last updated: May 4, 2026 · Effective for all users on or after May 4, 2026. By continuing to use Stratum after this date, you accept this policy.

This Privacy Policy explains how Stratum Intelligence ("Stratum," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use stratumwealth.ca and our newsletter services. We are committed to handling your data lawfully and transparently under Canadian privacy laws — specifically PIPEDA (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act) and CASL (Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation).

01Who We Are

Stratum Intelligence is operated by Stratum Wealth, based in Canada. We publish a market intelligence newsletter aimed at retail investors. We are the data controller of any personal information you provide through our website and newsletter.

02What We Collect

When you sign up for our free or paid newsletter, we collect:

  • Email address — required, so we can send you the newsletter.
  • Name — optional on signup; collected automatically by Stripe if you upgrade to a paid plan.
  • Payment information — handled directly by Stripe if you subscribe to Plus or Quant. We never see or store your card number; we only receive a customer ID from Stripe.
  • IP address and basic request metadata — logged for security and abuse prevention (e.g., detecting fake signups).
  • Subscription status and history — your current tier (Free, Plus, Quant), whether you've used a free trial, and dates relevant to billing.

We do not collect your phone number, address, financial account details, or browsing history. We do not use third-party advertising trackers, and we do not run advertising on stratumwealth.ca.

03Why We Collect It

  • To send you the newsletter you signed up for.
  • To verify your email address (single-use confirmation link).
  • To process payments and manage your subscription if you're on a paid tier.
  • To prevent abuse — for example, blocking IPs that flood our signup form with fake addresses.
  • To respond to support requests if you email us.

By submitting your email through our signup form and accepting our Terms, you provide express consent under Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL) to receive commercial electronic messages from Stratum Wealth at the email address you provided, including newsletter content, product updates, and account-related notices. You may withdraw this consent at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email or by contacting stratumwealth@stratumwealth.ca. We will honor unsubscribe requests within 10 business days as required by CASL.

04Who We Share It With

We share the minimum amount of data necessary with the third-party services that power Stratum:

  • Resend — sends the newsletter and transactional emails on our behalf. Receives your email address and the email content. Resend may track whether emails are opened and whether links inside the email are clicked, which we use in aggregate to measure newsletter effectiveness. We do not use this data for advertising and do not share it with third parties.
  • Stripe — processes payments for paid tiers. Receives your email, name, and payment details directly from you (we don't pass your card number).
  • Supabase — hosts our database. Stores subscriber records and report archives.
  • Railway — hosts our application servers.
  • Anthropic — powers parts of our content analysis pipeline. We do not send your email address, name, or other personal identifiers to Anthropic. Our prompts to Anthropic's API contain only publicly available market data, our own analytical context, and content we are generating. Subscriber-identifying information is never included in any prompt.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone, ever. We don't share it with advertisers or data brokers. The only times we would disclose your data outside the providers above are: (a) if you explicitly ask us to, (b) to comply with a valid legal order from Canadian authorities, or (c) to protect against fraud or harm to other users.

05Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored on infrastructure operated by Supabase and Railway, which use data centers primarily in the United States. By using Stratum, you consent to your data being processed outside Canada. The vendors we use (Stripe, Resend, Supabase, Railway, Anthropic) all maintain industry-standard security practices and are subject to applicable privacy regulations.

06How Long We Keep It

  • Active subscribers — for as long as you remain subscribed.
  • Unsubscribed users — we retain your email in a suppression list so we don't accidentally email you again. You can request full deletion (see Section 8).
  • Payment records — kept for as long as Canadian tax law requires (typically 6 years).
  • Security logs — IP addresses and request metadata are kept up to 30 days, then auto-deleted.

07Cookies and Tracking

stratumwealth.ca does not currently use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising. We do not run Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any similar third-party tracker. The only "cookie-like" data is what your browser sets automatically when loading Google Fonts, which we use for typography. If we add analytics in the future, this policy will be updated and you'll be notified.

08Your Rights

Under PIPEDA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal record-keeping requirements for paid subscribers).
  • Withdraw consent at any time by unsubscribing — every newsletter email contains an unsubscribe link.
  • File a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada if you believe we've mishandled your data.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at stratumwealth@stratumwealth.ca. We'll respond within 30 days.

09Security

We protect your data with HTTPS encryption, strict server-side input validation, rate limiting, IP-level abuse detection, and database row-level security. Payment data is handled exclusively by Stripe (PCI-DSS compliant) and never touches our servers in raw form. Despite our best efforts, no internet service can guarantee absolute security. If a data breach affecting your personal information occurs, we will notify you and the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, in accordance with PIPEDA's breach-notification requirements.

10Children

Stratum is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a child has signed up, contact us and we'll delete the account.

11Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our service evolves. Material changes will be communicated via email to active subscribers at least 30 days before taking effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

12Changes to Subprocessors

The third-party services listed in Section 04 (Resend, Stripe, Supabase, Railway, Anthropic) may change as our infrastructure evolves. We will update this Privacy Policy when we add, remove, or replace a subprocessor. Material changes affecting how your data is handled will be communicated by email at least 30 days in advance.
